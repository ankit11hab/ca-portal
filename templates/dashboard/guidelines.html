Submitting a POC

  • POC (Point Of Contact) - A person who acts on behalf of an organisation's club, society or team.
  • Make Alcher bigger than ever by helping us reach out to the clubs and societies of those schools, colleges, universities, and performing art studios (for instance, dance, music studios, drama schools) with whom you have contact.
  • Submit the point of contact in submission, and Team Alcheringa will be reaching out to them.
  • The scoring criteria for submitting a POC depends on the colleges, cubs, societies you made Alcher reach. Hence, a single and perfect POC is reasonably enough to fetch you a score rather than submitting multiple POC’s of the same clubs and societies.
  • Team Alcheringa will reward you with points for submitting a POC after confirming their genuineness and notify you.
  • Submitting a Data Responsibility Memo.
  • Use of the information provided by you: We only use the information you give to identify individuals, reach them, and keep them updated about Alcheringa through their mail and contact number. We do not share your information with any third parties.
  • Sharing of the information provided by you: We will not, in any circumstances, share the information you give with other individuals or organizations, including public organizations, corporations or individuals. We do not sell, communicate or divulge your information to any mailing lists.
  • Access to and Viewing your personal information: You can, at any time, access the personal information given by you under the profile section from the Dashboard page.

If you have any or comments regarding this Memorandum, don't hesitate to contact us.
