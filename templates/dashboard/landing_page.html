Who is an Alcheringa Campus Ambassador?

Campus Ambassadors are the key players of IIT Guwahati's Alcheringa capacity building and youth outreach programme. With the insight of bringing out the essence of an authentic leader within you . Along with a host of valuable experiences and reassurance from Team Alcheringa, come forward and take up the opportunity to plan , organise and execute exciting events. Join a community that engulfs you with moments that refines your managerial and communicative skills.

What will be your responsibilities?

College Branding

Take the lead in your college for Alcheringa and actively publicise us around you by using your promoting and showcasing skills. Take the chance and give professional presentations to key clubs and social groups on your Campus.

City Eliminations

Be the one in the lead to bring out the star performer among the rest of your city. Plan, Organise and be a part of Alcheringa's executive team to smoothen the flow of City elimination.

Increasing Reach

Use your rapport skills to make Alcheringa bigger than ever by helping us reach different cultural societies of colleges (Music, Drama, Dance etc..).

FAQs

Students from all colleges across the globe are welcome to apply.

You can apply through our website as soon as the portal is active. You will be notified after you successfully register. Then you can start contributing and working immediately on your tasks.

As a part of the program, you start at Novice level and complete various tasks such as Submitting contacts of individuals or groups of participants, increasing social media engagement, strategizing and branding on campus.

There are 4 levels in total (Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum), after successful completion of tasks in each level and earning enough points you would get upgraded to the next level

Yes, multiple students can join the Campus Ambassador program from one college. You can join and work as a team consisting of one senior and a junior.

There is no fee to participate in the Campus Ambassador Program; it's entirely free!

On average, you should put in 2 hours every week. The core aspects are commitment and consistency.

You will be the alcheringa'22 spokesperson at your institution and the first point of contact for any concerns that your college students may have

In addition to the incentives, awards, and the certification, it aids in the expansion of your network both on and off campus, as well as the improvement of your management and communication abilities.

City eliminations are basically Alcher Prelims conducted in major cities such as Delhi, Lucknow, Bhopal, etc, to bring out the best talent in these regions. The winners of these city eliminations will be getting travel reimbursement and exciting prizes to come participate in North East’s biggest Cultural Fest Alcheringa.
